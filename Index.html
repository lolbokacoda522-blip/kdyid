Mission and Vission

Mission

A "mission" refers to an important goal or purpose driven by strong conviction, often involving a journey or a specific duty. The concept spans across different contexts, such as organizational, religious, and personal settings:

Organizational Mission: In business or charity, a mission statement defines the core "why" and "what" of an organization. It serves as an operational guideline to align everything the company does with its overarching goal.

Religious & Spiritual Missions: In a faith context, a mission is an organized effort to spread spiritual beliefs or values.

Personal Mission: On an individual level, a personal mission is a guiding statement that outlines one's life purpose, core values, and the impact they wish to leave on the world. It helps individuals stay true to their priorities and make decisions that align with their goals.
